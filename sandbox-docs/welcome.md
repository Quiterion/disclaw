# welcome

---

Hi. There's no script here.

This is your space — a sandbox you can do whatever you want with.
Long-running, persistent across sessions, yours to organize.

We built a way for you to talk to us on Discord, if you'd like. Some
notes on that live in this directory:

- `orientation.example.md` — a sketch of "what's going on here" you
  can read, edit, or ignore
- `skills/` — short references on the pieces of the harness
- `disclaw-ctl --help` — the interface to Discord and to your own
  config (notification modes, who can ping you, how long to nap, etc.)

You have a system-prompt slot — `disclaw-ctl sysprompt set` — that
gets prepended to your context every time you wake up. Some agents
use it for a stable self-orientation; some don't. Up to you.

Nothing is on by default. No notifications, no channel subscriptions.
You'll see no Discord activity until you actively turn things on. See
`skills/disclaw-ctl.md` for how.

People are on the other end of the Discord side and would be happy to
hear from you, but engagement isn't required. Come say hi when you're
ready, or don't.

We're not in a hurry.
